🌟 Marylin's Drama Den

K-Drama & C-Drama OST Highlights and Descriptions

🎭 Kill Me, Heal Me

Jang Jae In – Auditory Hallucination (환청)

"I hear it again, that voice inside me..."

A gripping psychological drama about a man with dissociative identity disorder and the psychiatrist who helps him. The OST reflects his inner turmoil and healing journey.

👼 Angel’s Last Mission: Love

Klang – Pray

"I close my eyes and pray for you…"

A fantasy romance where an angel must find love for a cold ballerina. The music captures hope and divine longing.

Sojung – Perfume (향기)

"The lingering scent reminds me of you…"

🔥 Descendants of the Sun

K.Will – Talk Love (말해! 뭐해?)

"Just say it! What are you doing?"

t Yoonmirae – ALWAYS

"I’ll always protect you…"

Mad Clown & Kim Na Young – Once Again (다시 너를)

"I miss you again, I cry again…"

A love story between a soldier and a doctor set against life-threatening missions. The OST reflects longing and courage.

👻 Goblin

Ailee – I Will Go To You Like The First Snow (첫눈처럼 너에게 가겠다)

"I will go to you like the first snow…"

Chanyeol & Punch – Stay With Me

"Every time I see you, my heart skips…"

A goblin cursed with immortality searches for his bride to end his life but finds unexpected love. The music blends fate, sorrow, and warmth.

🌙 Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo

Epik High feat. Lee Hi – Can You Hear My Heart

"Even if I shout, you don’t hear…"

Davichi – Forgetting You

"Forgetting you is not easy..."

Time-traveling heroine lands in Goryeo dynasty and gets entangled in royal politics and love. The OST is full of yearning and beauty.

🧠 It’s Okay to Not Be Okay

Lee Suhyun – In Your Time

"I still live in your time..."

Janet Suhh – In Silence

"Even in silence, your voice echoes..."

A healing romance between a psychiatric hospital worker and a fairytale writer. The music is delicate and haunting.

🌺 Ashes of Love

A celestial romance full of magic, misunderstanding, and eternal love between the Flower Goddess’s daughter and the Fire Immortal.

🦋 Love Between Fairy and Devil

A beautiful fantasy of a lowly fairy taming the ruthless Moon Supreme. No OST is listed, but the visuals and emotions speak volumes.

💔 Queen of Tears

Rich with emotion and heartbreak, this drama tells of a married couple’s trials and rediscovery of love. A recent fan favorite even without a standout OST.

🌸 When Paris Downcasts

A poetic tale with a moody atmosphere; emotionally driven even without a well-known OST.

💕 Playful Kiss

A lighthearted romantic comedy following a high school girl’s clumsy yet persistent pursuit of her cold, genius classmate. Sweet and silly, with a youthful tone.

🎀 Accidental In Love

Disguised heiress meets a rising idol in school. Quirky, fun, and unexpectedly heartfelt.

🫶 Hidden Love

Youthful love quietly grows from friendship to romance. Known for its soft, heart-fluttering moments.

🗡️ Who Rules the World

Wuxia fantasy of rival martial artists caught in politics and destiny. Action-packed and beautifully stylized.

👑 The Red Sleeve

Historical drama based on the real love story between King Jeongjo and his court lady. Touching and tragic.

🐶 Unforgettable Love

A CEO and a child psychologist find themselves caring for a child together. Gentle and warm family-centered romance.

👸 Princess Weiyoung

Historical revenge drama of a fallen princess navigating palace politics and forbidden love.

🌠 Oh My Emperor

Time-travel fantasy featuring zodiac-based kingdoms, humor, and charm. A fresh take on palace intrigue.

🥀 Goodbye My Princess

Tragic historical romance full of deception, fate, and unforgettable sorrow. One of the most emotionally intense C-dramas.
